strongprivate

Move generator type.

Enumerator
NO_CHECK 

Move generator for when the king is not in check. All moves are considered.

CHECK 

Move generator for when the king is in check (single). King moves are considered first, castling moves are not considered at all, and regular piece moves must either capture the checker or intercept the check.

DOUBLE_CHECK 

Move generator for when the king is in double check. Only king moves are considered.

Applies a move on the current position. The move is assumed to be legal, and it must be from one of the generators. No legality checks are done by this function.

Parameters
[in]mMove to apply

This function does the following:

  • Updates bit boards, castling rights, and EP square (m_epSquare) as per the move
  • Updates m_kingSq
  • Updates half move clock (m_halfMoveClock) and ply number (m_plyNum)
    • Increase of m_plyNum changes the side to move
  • Swaps m_kingSq and m_oppKingSq
  • Flips turn color mask (m_turnColorMask)
  • Recalculates checkers (m_checkers)
